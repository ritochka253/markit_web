MarkIt

Introducing MarkIt - the ultimate app for adding watermarks to your photos with ease! The app offers a range of watermark options, including both text and image watermarks. You can customise your watermarks with text, logos, or any image of your choice. You can also adjust the watermark's size, pattern as well as change its opacity and angle, to achieve the perfect effect.
MarkIt also offers text colour options, enabling you to choose the perfect hue to match your branding or aesthetic. The app's user-friendly interface makes it easy to edit and save your watermarked photos. Whether you're a professional photographer, an artist, or simply looking to add a personal touch to your social media posts, MarkIt is the app you need to make your photos stand out.
